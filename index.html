What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is all about making computers think like people! It's a big part of computer science where we teach machines to do smart things that usually need a human brain.

This includes things like learning new facts, solving puzzles, understanding your voice, and even seeing things (like your phone's face lock). The main goal is for computers to work smart and on their own. Things you use every day, like Siri, Alexa, or even a self-driving car, all use AI!

Machine Learning (ML)

Machine Learning (ML) is the main way we teach AI to be smart. Think of it like teaching a puppy! Instead of giving the computer a huge list of rules, we give it tons of examples (data).

The computer then studies this data, finds patterns all by itself, and gets better at making predictions over time. For example, ML learns what a dog looks like by seeing thousands of pictures of dogs, not by being told exactly where the ears and tail are. ML is the engine that helps AI improve every single day!
